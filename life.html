Policy Terms and Conditions

Applicants must be between 18 and 60 years of age at the time of policy purchase.

The policy is available to both salaried and self-employed individuals.

The policy provides life coverage for a duration of 16 years from the date of commencement.

Premiums must be paid on time, with the frequency of monthly payment.

A grace period of 30 days is allowed for missed payments, after which the policy may lapse.

Claims will not be entertained for death due to suicide within the first year of the policy.

Pre-existing medical conditions not disclosed during policy issuance will void the coverage.

Surrendering the policy before completion of the term is allowed only after 3 years of continuous premium payments.

The surrender value will be calculated based on the total premiums paid and applicable deductions.

The policyholder can assign or update their nominee at any time through the customer support portal.

In the event of the policyholder's demise, the nominee must submit the claim along with valid documents.

Claims will be processed within 7 working days, subject to verification.

In case of severe physical disability due to accident or illness, future premiums will be waived while maintaining the full coverage benefits.

Upon completing the policy term, the policyholder will receive a maturity benefit of up to ₹3 lakhs, provided all premiums have been paid.

Policyholders can cancel within the free-look period of 15 days from policy issuance for a full refund.

After the free-look period, cancellation will be subject to applicable deductions.

Policy issuance and claim settlements require completion of eKYC verification.

Lapsed policies can be revived within 2 years from the last unpaid premium, subject to health reassessment and payment of dues.
